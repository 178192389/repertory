ABOUT US

Shenzhen Branch of the Xin Electronics Co., Ltd. is an integrated circuit software and hardware research and development-oriented high-tech enterprises. Focusing on consumer electronics, wireless charging, wearing equipment, health care, mobile terminals and other emerging areas to provide high-end chip and power solutions, the company has accumulated strong technical, professional production / sales power management IC, wireless charging transmitter / Pressure LDO, boost IC, step-down IC, LED driver IC, voltage detection IC, lithium battery charge management IC, MOS tube, and other products.

Embodies a vibrant, positive and progressive, innovative R & D and management team, most of its engineering and technical personnel in the integrated circuit industry has more than 10 years of experience, at any time to provide professional services to customers.

Our goal is to be a high-quality product, efficient service, to meet the growing needs of customers, and strive to become the industry's leading supplier of integrated circuits.
